THRUSTY CROW



There was a crow that was very thrusty in the summer season. The crow was searching for water very badly .He flew everywhere in the city but was unable to find water anywhere.But after sometime he reach into a village where that crow found a matka half filled of water.The crow badly needed this water in any ways,when that crow put his mouth into that matka he was unable to reach to that water in it.Many time he put his mouth to drink water but he failed.After sometime he used his mind and put the stone into the matka so that the water comes up in the matka.Then he happily drink water and flew away.
